#### B. Cold Calling

**Why:** Fastest path to a conversation. Most solo consultants avoid it, which means less noise. A 30-second regulation-led opener cuts through.

**Pre-Call Research (2 min per call):**
- Check their website for any AI/ML/digital transformation language
- Check LinkedIn for recent hires or posts about AI
- Check if they're regulated (PIPEDA, OSFI, PHIPA, ITSG-33)
- Note one specific thing to reference in the opener

**Cold Call Opener (30 seconds):**
```
Hi [First], this is [First name] with Elect-Rix in Moncton. I'll be brief — 
I work with [credit unions / law firms / health orgs] in New Brunswick on keeping 
AI data on their own infrastructure. [Org name] is [regulated / handling 
sensitive client data], and I know [PIPEDA / OSFI / PHIPA] compliance is top 
of mind right now with generative AI entering the workplace.

I'm not selling a platform — I deploy private AI on your hardware and verify 
it stays private. Would a 15-minute call this week be useful, or is there a 
better person on your team to speak with?
```

**Gatekeeper Navigation:**
```
"I'm calling about data sovereignty compliance for [org]'s AI initiatives. 
It's regarding PIPEDA requirements for on-premise solutions. [Decision-maker's 
name] would understand the context."
```

**Voicemail Script (15 seconds):**
```
[First], this is [First name] with Elect-Rix. I help [regulated orgs / 
credit unions / law firms] keep AI data on-premise and PIPEDA-compliant. 
One question for you: has [org] evaluated what happens to client data when 
staff use cloud-based AI tools? I'm at [number]. Happy to share what other 
NB organizations are doing. Thanks.
```

**Objection Handling:**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We're not looking at AI right now" | "Understood. Most of my clients said the same — until they found out their staff were already using cloud AI tools with client data. The risk isn't adopting AI, it's uncontrolled adoption. Worth a 15-min assessment?" |
| "We use [cloud tool]" | "That's common. The question is: where does that data go? Most cloud AI tools process data in US data centers. I help orgs run AI locally so data never leaves your infrastructure." |
| "We have an IT team for that" | "Great — I work alongside internal IT. Most teams I see have the skills to manage AI but need help with the deployment architecture and verification. I hand off a system your team can run independently." |
| "Send me an email" | "Absolutely. What's the best address? I'll send a one-page overview with the 5 PIPEDA gaps I see most often in [their sector]." |

**Volume:** 15-20 calls/day, 3 days/week. Target: 3-5 conversations/week, 1-2 meetings/week.

**Call Blocks:**
- Tuesday 9:00-11:30 AM
- Wednesday 1:00-3:30 PM
- Thursday 9:00-11:30 AM

Monday = prep day (research + emails). Friday = follow-ups + content.

## 8. WHAT NOT TO DO

1. **Don't lead with product.** Lead with regulation + risk. The buyer already knows AI is entering their org. They don't know the data risk.
2. **Don't sell cloud alternatives.** You're not anti-cloud. You're pro-verification. Frame as "cloud has its place — but not for regulated data without verification."
3. **Don't name competitor products.** No "instead of ChatGPT" or "better than Claude." Generic terms only: "cloud AI tools," "subscription AI."
4. **Don't discount the Assessment.** It's $3,500 minimum. The value is the audit findings. Discounting signals low value. Instead, offer a free 15-minute AI risk scan (teaser, not the full assessment).
5. **Don't chase outside Atlantic Canada yet.** Focus on NB first (drive distance), then NS. National reach comes through case studies and content, not outbound.
6. **Don't frame as "small business."** Frame as "independent verification" and "specialist deployment." You're a specialist, not a small agency.
7. **Don't skip the partner channel.** Aatio + QuantumBridge + MSP referrals could produce 30-50% of leads by month 6. Invest the relationship time now.
8. **Don't confuse "tin cup" with strategy.** ACOA funding is leverage, not survival. Revenue is the plan. Funding accelerates — it doesn't substitute.
